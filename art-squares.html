Art Squares

As a way of commemorating progress in the everyday project, I am creating a collage of the art pieces for every square day (1, 4, 9, 16, etc.). As much as I would love to post the multi-GB files that are up to 50,000px wide, I have bandwidth considerations, so each art square has been resized to 6000x6000 pixels. Each square is made by sequentially stitching the daily art pieces together from left to right then from top to bottom. Enjoy!
